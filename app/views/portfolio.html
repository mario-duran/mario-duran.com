Crack my Screen

iOS app

The Crack my Screen App is a fun way to trick your friends into thinking you cracked your iPhone screen.
Simply, open the App and select a background picture or select the default iPhone background and press the screen to 'Crack' the screen.
If you have an iPhone 6s / 6s Plus with 3D touch enabled more elaborate and scientific screen cracking will be available.

Sadly this app didn'e made it to the app store because it wasn't compliant with the Apple's xyz policies.
